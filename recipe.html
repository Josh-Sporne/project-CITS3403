🍝 Creamy Pasta

Creator: Jamie

Rating: ⭐ 4.8

Cooking Time: ⏱ 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • • pasta
  • • garlic
  • • cream
  • • parmesan
  • • olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add cream and simmer.
  4. Mix in parmesan and cooked pasta.
  5. Serve hot with extra cheese.
